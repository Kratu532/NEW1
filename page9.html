Welcome! We're happy you're here. 

One day I (Sady) and my friend (Bety) thought about writing stories and publishing it online and hence Kratu was born. We always loved reading books and writing one was a tempting idea so we set on to work. Gradually we created the platform and set on to writing.

But we always wished a helping hand. We know that you also have written a story someday and wished you could publish it. The way of publishing a hard cover book is a long and tedious task, so why not publish one online. We are waiting for your original works, be it a poem or a short story or a long one doesn't matter. What we want is just a story or poem, that's all!

You can send us your work on this email: kratu.online532@gmail.com

You can write in any genre: sci-fi, mystery, fantasy, mythology, factual, educational etc. 

Please be original. We have an editorial system, so no offensive materials as they will be discarded and not published.
